Sales Closer

Location: Remote Type: Commission / Full-Time

Overview

Launch Lords is looking for a Sales Closer to handle qualified conversations with healthcare business owners interested in implementing AI-powered systems.

This role focuses on understanding operational problems, communicating system capabilities, and closing deals with decision-makers. You will primarily work with inbound or pre-qualified prospects interested in automation, AI reception systems, and conversion infrastructure.

Responsibilities

  • Conduct discovery calls with healthcare business owners
  • Identify operational bottlenecks in lead capture and communication workflows
  • Present AI system solutions clearly and effectively
  • Guide prospects through the decision-making process
  • Close deals and coordinate onboarding with internal teams
  • Maintain accurate pipeline and deal tracking

Requirements

  • Experience in consultative sales or closing roles
  • Strong communication and objection-handling skills
  • Ability to understand operational business problems
  • Comfortable leading structured sales conversations
  • Self-motivated and comfortable working remotely

Performance Expectations

  • Consistently convert qualified prospects into paying clients
  • Maintain strong communication with prospects during the decision cycle
  • Provide feedback from sales conversations to improve messaging and positioning
